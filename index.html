Exhibition's Portal is a Project to bring people closer to museum's worldwide. A solid databse of everything that is happening in museum's all around the world will guarantee you that wherever you are / going to will have a lot to see and to explore. The Project aims to bring everyone closer to the art and everything a museum has to offer, including monuments, gardens, places people etc.

The funding is for helping build the Portal in about 5000$ and more 5000$ for aditional costs in promoting the platform and bringing people and data into the site. Coordination between the team and the museum's will be a fundamental factor.
